Having the full wish to write every day, is not at all, all to write everyday.

Oct 14, 2017 • Arnab Das • #100-words

Having the full wish to write every day, is not at all, all to write everyday. It takes a determination of different level. So after the start of writing spree, it has been quite a long time since the last entry. And a couple of entries are also back dated. At that time, it almost felt like doing pending homework. So discontinued. Writing doesn’t come as homework.

Since the last entry lot of things happened. Taken a few small projects personally. All of them discontinued due to lack of enthusiasm for running for a small duration. Some of the efforts included get up early and doing exercises also. Joke apart, personal projects, any type of project needs a lot of determination. No doubt. And the individuals, who finish, suppose open source or say runs some kind of common interest groups, are really respectable, admirable. Just now came across a post in Medium about starting. Good to read. Nothing new. But nicely arranged. “Starting things is scary.”.

Went to Kolkata during Durga puja. Saw a lot of familiar faces. Actually seeing someone after a long time feels somewhat warm. Can’t describe exactly. But warm. Even the person whom I didn’t like in the past, seems, what I can say, ‘lukewarm’?
