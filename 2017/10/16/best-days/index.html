Best Days In My Life

Best days does not come along the way. We have to carve lazy, not-so-good days out into the most memorable ones

Oct 16, 2017 • Arnab Das • #100-words

Just now watched three movies back to back She’s Out Of My League, Good Luck Chuck and Young Adult. Man, what a depth she has. Charliz Theron. She is the most attractive, most sensitive, most empathetic. She is the best among three.

Though my school life was not that memorable. But those days feels very good. I never dated in school. In college. Not even in University. But the school and University both feels good. Both of the time didn’t feel the peer pressure for getting someone. And in fact University was great. Best days of my life.

I can completely relate with Marvis Gary. Completely. Though I’m not that successful as her. But both of us has spend their most memorable days. Best days are gone. Long long gone. But still we are looking for some days in future to make them part of our best ones. Though I’m not sure how. But there is no harm in wishing some good days for us.

Life I’m coming.
