Watching NCIS

Oct 15, 2017 • Arnab Das • #100-words

Recently I’ve been watching NCIS. It’s just not a TV series, it’s a kind of journey of life. It’s adherence to pure principles of life makes it unique among the likes. The characters with their evolution through each episodes have been remarkably sketched with great care. Apart from exerting extraordinary efforts towards their duty, they are human, infact too much. When as a viewer we expect something heroic, they exhibit painstakingly common persona while doing their job. So they fail, they make mistakes. But they can’t imagine to leave behind a fellow traveler, whether be a marine or a most commoner. Everyone gets chance, importance. The way they should. It’s no miracle, it’s the most watched criminal investigation series in USA.
