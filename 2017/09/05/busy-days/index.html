Busy Days

Some days don't leave any perceptible impression on our mind, that it has gone, passed by. I like this kind of days. Nothing new happened. Even didn't even read an article.

Sep 05, 2017 • Arnab Das • #100-words

Some days don’t leave any perceptible impression on our mind, that it has gone, passed by. Today was like this. I could not be myself for the whole day. And it just passed by. It’s not that, I worked very hard today. But somehow it remained unfelt. But I like this kind of days. Nothing new happened. Even didn’t even read an article. Yes, I watched the news for some time. But news content is so cliche that the word cliche feels like more cliche, mundane. There is no newness in news. Only destruction, corruption, falling humanity. I really doubt the sanity of the mind of the news reader and the journalists nowadays. Really what news they have to cover, then analyze and then read. I’ve seen them analyzing whether a political person has taken the bribe for own party or personal benefit. Bribes are also being categorized currently. What is going to happen to this ill-fated country? Huh.
