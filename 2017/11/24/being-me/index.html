Being Me

Don't be selfish. Be what you are. Feel what you are. I'm lucky that, I've a family. Even if you don't have a family, be for yourself. You should long for yourself.

Nov 24, 2017 • Arnab Das • #100-words

Year after year goes by. Growing older each year by one year. I am thankful a lot of people, that they are letting me to grow up. Really. I should be thankful and I am.

Sometimes I ponder over one question, what if I’m not there. Not here. Anywhere. Nowhere. What would happen? Would anybody notice my absense? Is there anyone, who would actually feel the void of me not around anymore.

I’ve my parents. They are going to feel the void. Yes. They will. For the first somedays, it would be an insufferable pain. After that, presumably, they will try to cope up with the situation. And if they are unlucky enough to live a long life, they will learn to live without me.

But, is it right for me. Obviously not. They looked after me, when I was weak, when I was sick. Now, I don’t live with them, but they have the assurance that, I’m there and I’m for them when they need. Even if they don’t need, I’m there to be with them at least for sometime. This is what they look for, longing for, throughout the whole year. And I’m in no place to be nowhere for them. I’ve to be always there for them.

Don’t be selfish. Be what you are. Feel what you are. I’m lucky that, I’ve a family. Even if you don’t have a family, be for yourself. You should long for yourself. Actually that is more liberating. There are no obligations for you. So, you can do whatever you want. Whatever. You have the liberty to come up with your real self.

Very few gets the chance to be you. Whatever you are. Whatever your situation may be. You are you. This is a blessing. So be you. Be happy.
