As I Am Now

Jul 06, 2018 • Arnab Das • #100-words

After realizing the fact, for an unknown reason, a meaningful purpose has completely taken over me. As I used to say, and also written in some of my earlier blogs, even in this site, rather complained about losing motivation. Now, it’s thoroughly gone. Now I know I’ve to continue at least for the next 24/25 years in order to support the child. It’s actually amazing to have someone to get going. May be I can also make progress in some of the streams I’ve been thinking about for a long time. But again it’s very unfortunate that I can’t go outside the influence of my family. Family is required. But one should be ready to work on his/her own. Then the opportunity of actual improvement can be envisaged. Nothing should influence a person so much, that may sketch the path of life. I can’t deny the requirement for insiparion to go on in tough situations and I got one. That’s the good news after all.
