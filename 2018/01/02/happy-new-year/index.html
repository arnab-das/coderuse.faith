Happy New Year

A great author once said, it's very difficult to walk here for those, who have even a feeling of 'there'

Jan 02, 2018 • Arnab Das • #100-words

Year passes by. Passes by the time, I want to believe in. The very question of whether to continue or not, remains with a glowy aura around. In despair, at the ends of paths, whenever I think of betraying the natural course of life, a wall of very rigid sense comes in the way to freedom. A great author once said, it’s very difficult to walk here for those, who have even a feeling of ‘there’. Didn’t think of much, when was not ready. But now it feels truth at it’s best. Each day passes as the rolling of tightly swindled rope around a weary body. There is no energy remaining to initiate the exit. So the only course is to think of ‘there’ and just continue. Just continue. Nothing else. Like the travellers, almost at the end of their destination, the last point is seen at the corner of the road. Accumulating the last drops of hope by seeing the destination at a distance. And with the hope, just walking, continuing the natural course of life. What else.

Happy New Year?
